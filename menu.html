Dish Descriptions:

Beyond with Cheese

flame-grilled Beyond Burger, cooked to perfection, with melted vegan cheese and all the delicious toppings you could ask for. This masterpiece is elevated to new heights, making every bite a truly unforgettable experience.

Pizza:

Our pizzas are hand-tossed, ensuring a truly unique and flavorful experience in every bite. We use only the freshest toppings—never frozen—like cauliflower, onion, green pepper, and broccoli, all laid over a rich red sauce. With every slice, you'll discover something new and delicious.

GREEN GODDESS CLUB:

Our club sandwich is a fan favorite, featuring a creamy avocado-based mayonnaise and stacked with tender, plant-based chicken strips that are full of flavor. Add in fresh tomatoes, crisp lettuce, and tangy pickles, you have the perfect, most delicious sandwich for a lunch on the move.

RAINBOW BUDDHA BOWL:

Our Rainbow Buddha Bowl is a vibrant, nourishing dish that’s as colorful as it is delicious. Packed with fresh, wholesome ingredients, it features a mix of crispy vegetables, hearty grains, and protein-rich options. Topped with a sweet and spicy dressing and a pinch of sunflower seeds, each bite delivers a balance of textures and flavors, making it the perfect energizing meal for any time of day.

FRUIT SALAD:

Our Fruit Salad is a customer favorite for its simplicity and natural sweetness. Featuring a fresh blend of watermelon, grapes, pineapple, honeydew, cantaloupe, and banana, it’s topped with a light drizzle of maple syrup that perfectly balances sweet and savory. A refreshing and delicious choice, loved by all.

PREMIUM ROMAIN SALAD:

The name says it all: this salad features large, crisp flakes of Romaine lettuce, chopped tomatoes, and fresh cabbage, making it the perfect quick snack or pre-meal appetizer. Served with a side of salsa, it offers a fresh, flavorful twist that opens the door to exciting new flavor combinations.

HUMMUS PLATTER:

A bowl of our fresh, house-made hummus, served alongside crisp carrots, broccoli, radishes, turnips, and other seasonal vegetables. This light and refreshing dish is the perfect pick-me-up on a hot summer day.

CAULIFLOWER WINGS:

Crushed cauliflower is carefully pressed together, then coated in a rich mixture of vegan butter and breadcrumbs, creating a satisfying crunch. Air-fried to golden perfection, this dish offers a deliciously healthy option that's full of flavor and texture. With its vibrant appearance and irresistible taste, it’s an eye-catching choice that’s sure to stand out at any meal.

STUFFED MUSHROOMS:

Chanterelles are expertly stuffed with a silky puree of okra, eggplant, and artichoke, then finished with a delicate drizzle of champagne vinaigrette. This daring and bold dish pushes the boundaries of culinary creativity, offering a unique flavor profile that challenges what food can truly be. It's truly exceptional for those seeking something unforgettable.
