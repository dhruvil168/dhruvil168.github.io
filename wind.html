Are Wind Turbines Right for You?

Not all homes are suitable for turbines, it is important to consider these factors before installing:

1. Climate

This will be the biggest factor that will effect your wind turbine fficiency. If your house is located in a windy climate then wind turbines will be extreemly efficient and the best option. However, if you are located in a climate with more sunshine, turbines will be sub optimal. Instead, you might want to check out solar panels

2. Costs

Every wind turbine is diffferent but they mostly range from $200-$1000 each. Most turbines can be intalled with the included manual so ther will be no need to hire a contractor!
