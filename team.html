- Meet the Team -

Head Chef: Danny

Danny studied under Giada De Laurentiis in Italy in 2015, upon his return home he started as a Sous-chef

Danny is an accomplished chef who had the privilege of studying under the renowned Giada De Laurentiis in Italy in 2015. During his time in Italy, he honed his skills in the art of Italian cuisine, gaining invaluable knowledge of both traditional techniques and contemporary approaches. This immersive experience allowed him to master the nuances of authentic Italian flavors, seasonal ingredients, and culinary traditions.

Upon returning home, Danny began his career as a sous chef, where he worked closely with the head chef to oversee kitchen operations, refine menus, and develop new dishes. His background in Italy equipped him with a distinctive culinary perspective, allowing him to blend classic Italian influences with modern innovations. Through dedication and expertise, Danny continued to elevate the craft, gaining recognition for his creativity, precision, and sophisticated approach to cooking.

Cook: Darnell

Darnell has been honing his culinary skills since the age of 18, bringing a wealth of experience and creativity to our kitchen. He masterfully combines flavorful dishes with passion and integrity, always ensuring the highest quality in every creation. Darnell's dedication to excellence is evident in every plate he prepares, as he meticulously crafts each dish with care and precision.

His innovative approach to cooking allows him to blend traditional techniques with modern flavors, creating a unique dining experience that delights our guests. Darnell's commitment to using fresh, high-quality ingredients ensures that every meal is delicious and nourishing. His ability to infuse his dishes with heart and soul makes him the star player of our establishment, earning the admiration and respect of colleagues and patrons alike.

Whether experimenting with new recipes or perfecting classic favorites, Darnell's culinary artistry shines through, making every dining experience memorable and exceptional.

Manager: Ravi

Ravi brings over 15 years of experience in the restaurant industry, making him exceptionally prepared to handle any situation that comes his way. With his seasoned expertise and steady leadership, Ravi serves as a "guiding light" for our team, as Danny likes to say. His ability to inspire and direct ensures the team operates at its best every day.

Waitress: Hera

Hera brings a compassionate approach to our clients, offering a fresh and inviting style to our establishment. We are thrilled to have her as part of our journey and look forward to supporting her as she continues to grow and gain experience in the industry.
