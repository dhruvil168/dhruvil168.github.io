About Us

Our mission: to help homeowners make the right decision for their homes and the environment

GreenHome is a website designed to help homeowners reduce their carbon footprint. Our platform offers a streamlined experience where you can find information on various eco-friendly solutions like solar panels, wind turbines, electric vehicles, and charities. So why wait? Start exploring today!

Get Started:

There are overwhelming amounts of green energy products and options for homeowners. We have complied a brief overview of the most prominent ones to help you get started!

Solar Panels:

Solar panels are the most reliable green energy option. They work everywhere and output a consitent output of energy to your home.

Wind Turbines:

Wind turbines are a great options for larger homes. They output high levels of energy and last long.

Electric Vehicles:

Electric vehicles are the way to go if you want a low cost way to lower your carbon footprint. Electric vehicles have the most options in terms of customization.
